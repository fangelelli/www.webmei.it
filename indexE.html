Who was Livia Drusilla ?

She was born in 58 b.C.. She was the daugther of Marco Livio Druso and when she was about 15 years old she married Tiberio Claudio Nerone.

Woman

She was a flawless wife and a skilled chef. She personally worked her own kitchen garden in her manor of Prima Porta. She had no sons from the emperor, but he never divorced probably because Augusto was really in love with her. Anyway he was secure to have already an offspring from his first wife Scribonia. Unfortunately no one of the Augusto grandchildren survived and the power went to Tiberio, the second son of Livia. The first one Druso mggiore died in war.

Salutist

They say that Livia suggeted, as a longevity secret, a good glass of wine at meal. It is good to remember that Livia lived up to 86 years. She was frugal and did not mixed wines, she used just one 'brand', Il Pucino, It was produced by a higly selected vineyard and it was produced not far from the Timavo river mouth. It is traceable to our Prosecco. She was careful of body care and she was a follower of the medicine doctor Asclepiade. He prescribed diets, massages, passive training, bathing and a little drinking of wine. This because
