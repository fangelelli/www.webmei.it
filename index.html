Our Story

2012Founded

Born from a belief in
meaningful objects

Lumina was founded in 2012 in a small studio in Copenhagen. We started with one question: why do the objects we use most deserve the least attention?

Since then, we've grown into an international team of 40 designers, engineers, and craftspeople — united by an obsession with quality and a deep respect for the humans who use our products.
